Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

A Novel by J.K. Rowling

Chapter 1

The Other Minister - This chapter starts with the fall out from Voldemort's growing power from the previous Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix. The Minister of magic and the muggle Prime Minister have to work together.

Chapter 2

Spinner's End - Snape has made a vow to protect Draco at all costs. This promise and participation in the Unbreakable Vow with Draco's mother puts Snape's allegiance in question.

Chapter 3

An Excess of Phlegm - Harry arrives at the Weasley's home known as The Burrow.
